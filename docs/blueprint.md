# **App Name**: CoutureFlow ERP

## Core Features:

- Shopify Integration: Seamlessly synchronize product catalogs, customer data, orders, and inventory levels with Shopify storefronts to ensure real-time consistency.
- WMS Data Synchronization (via bipower): Integrate and harmonize inventory and fulfillment data from the existing Warehouse Management System via 'bipower' to maintain accurate stock and shipping information.
- Product & Catalog Management: A centralized hub for managing luxury women's clothing items, including detailed SKUs, variations, high-resolution imagery, rich product descriptions, and pricing across all channels.
- Order Fulfillment Dashboard: Provide a comprehensive dashboard for overseeing and managing the entire order lifecycle, from processing and packing to shipping and delivery updates.
- Multi-Role User Access: Define and assign specific user roles (e.g., Administrator, Merchandiser, Sales Manager, Warehouse Staff) with granular permissions tailored to their responsibilities.
- Smart Merchandising Assistant: An AI tool that suggests optimal product groupings, collection ideas, and enhanced product descriptions for marketing based on current inventory, sales trends, and luxury brand guidelines.
- Essential Analytics & Reporting: Generate critical reports on sales performance, inventory turnover, customer behavior, and operational efficiency to inform strategic business decisions.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A deep, muted purple (#47006B) to evoke luxury, creativity, and sophistication, providing a strong contrast with the dark background.
- Background color: A very dark, subtle purplish-grey (#19151A) creating an elegant and refined dark mode experience, emphasizing the luxurious nature of the brand.
- Accent color: A vibrant medium blue-violet (#8282FF) used sparingly for interactive elements, calls to action, and highlighting important data points to ensure visibility and modern appeal.
- All text uses 'Inter' (sans-serif), chosen for its exceptional readability across various screen sizes and its modern, professional aesthetic, suitable for both luxury branding and data-intensive ERP displays.
- Utilize minimalist, elegant line icons with subtle filled variations for active states. Icons should maintain a clean and sophisticated appearance, complementing the luxury brand.
- Implement a clean, structured dashboard layout with ample negative space to convey premium quality and ease of navigation. Employ responsive design principles for seamless access across devices.
- Incorporate subtle and smooth transitions for state changes, data loading, and modal interactions. Animations should enhance user experience without feeling distracting or slowing down the application.
